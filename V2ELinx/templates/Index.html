V2ELinx



Vivek Vidyarthi Examination (V2ELinx) is a platform designed to streamline examination management for students of standards 8, 9, and 10. Developed specifically for Sri Ramakrishna Ashrama in Mysore, this application facilitates efficient handling of student data, marks, attendance, and ranking calculations.

Features Overview:

  • Managing student information including register number, name, standard, marks, and total marks.
  • Adding, removing, updating, and viewing student details based on standard selection.
  • Setting attendance for each student.
  • Setting and viewing marks for individual subjects or total marks.
  • Calculating ranks based on standards (8, 9, 10) and school-wise.
  • Minimum passing marks requirement: 20 out of 100.
